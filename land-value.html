ℹ️ Key Rules for NRIs

  • ✓Stamp Duty: Flat 8% for all buyers in Kerala (no gender concession)
  • ✓Registration Fee: Additional 2% of property value
  • ✓NRI Purchase: No restrictions on residential or commercial property
  • ✗Agricultural Land: NRIs cannot buy agri / plantation land (FEMA)
  • ✓Power of Attorney: Apostilled POA valid for registration from abroad
  • ⚠TDS (NRI seller): Buyer must deduct 12.5-20%+ TDS. NRI seller can apply Form 13 to reduce this.
  • ✓Payment: Must be routed via NRE / NRO bank account only
