📍 What NRIs Must Know

  • ⚠ Market Price vs SRO Value: Stamp duty is levied on the higher of the actual sale price or SRO guideline value. Since NRIs typically buy at market price (2-3x SRO), they pay stamp duty on the full market price — not the SRO rate.
  • ✓ No Gender Concession: Kerala does not offer any reduced rate for women buyers. The same 8% applies to men, women, and joint purchases.
  • ✓ Payment Method: Stamp duty and registration fee must be paid via Demand Draft or e-challan at the Sub-Registrar Office. NRIs must route funds from NRE/NRO account.
  • ✓ Gift Deed Advantage: If receiving property from parents/siblings already in India, a Gift Deed (Blood Relative) at 2%+1% = 3% total is far cheaper than a Sale Deed at 10%.
  • ⚠ Undervaluation Penalty: If the registrar suspects undervaluation, they can refer the deed for market value assessment. Underpaying stamp duty can attract a penalty of 2% per month on the deficient amount.
  • ✓ Blood Relatives Covered: For reduced gift/settlement rates, blood relatives include spouse, parents, children, siblings, grandparents, grandchildren, and their spouses.
