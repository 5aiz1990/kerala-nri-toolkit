Kerala Gold Rate — Common Questions

What is 1 gram 22 carat gold rate in Kerala today?
The estimated 22 carat gold rate per gram in Kerala today is shown in the rate cards above. It is based on the international XAU spot price converted to INR plus an India import duty adjustment. The actual jeweller price at Malabar Gold, Joyalukkas, or Kalyan may vary by ₹100–₹300/gram. Use the calculator toggle to include making charges and 3% GST for a realistic estimate.
How many grams is 1 sovereign of gold in Kerala?
1 sovereign of gold in Kerala equals exactly 8 grams. This is the standard unit used across Kerala and South India for jewellery, dowry, and gold investment. Our calculator automatically shows the sovereign price alongside the per-gram price.
What is silver rate today in Kerala per gram?
Today's Kerala silver rate per gram and per 10 grams is shown in the silver cards above. Kerala silver prices now use a layered market estimate based on the international XAG spot price converted to INR, plus duty, GST, a small market premium, and a local Kerala adjustment.
How much gold can I bring from Dubai to Kerala duty-free?
NRIs returning to India from Dubai can bring gold jewellery duty-free up to 20 grams (max ₹50,000) for men and 40 grams (max ₹1,00,000) for women. Gold beyond this attracts 12.5% customs duty + 3% IGST. Gold bars and coins do not qualify for the duty-free allowance.
Why is the Kerala jeweller price different from the rate shown here?
The rates shown here are based on international spot prices plus an estimated import duty adjustment. The official Kerala rate is set daily by KGSMA (Kerala Gold & Silver Merchants Association) and may vary by ₹100–₹300/gram. Jewellers also add making charges (₹1,000–₹2,500 per sovereign) and 3% GST. Use the toggle in the calculator above for a closer estimate.
What is 1 dirham (AED) in Indian rupees today?
Today's AED to INR exchange rate is shown in our Exchange Rates tool. The Currency to Gold Calculator on this page also uses the live AED/INR rate to show exactly how many grams and sovereigns your dirhams can buy at today's Kerala gold rate.
