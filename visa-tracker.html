⚠ The 182-Day Tax Rule

If you stay in India for 182 or more days in a financial year (April–March), you automatically become a Resident Indian for tax purposes — even if you hold a Gulf visa. This means your worldwide income becomes taxable in India.

Track your India visits carefully. Consult a CA if your annual India stay is approaching 150+ days.

🌎 Gulf Visa Renewal — Key Facts for Keralites

✓ UAE Residence Visa: Valid for 1, 2 or 3 years (or 5-10 years on unsponsored). Emirates ID must match visa validity. Renewal takes 1-2 weeks — start 30 days before expiry. Source: u.ae official
✓ UAE Grace Period: After residence visa cancellation or expiry, residents are granted a flexible grace period of up to 6 months (varies by resident category) before fines of AED 50/day kick in. Check with ICA or GDRFA for your specific category. Source: u.ae official
⚠ UAE 180-Day Outside Rule: If you stay outside the UAE for more than 180 consecutive days, your residence visa is automatically nullified — even if it hasn't reached its expiry date. Critical for NRIs visiting India for extended periods. Source: u.ae official
✓ Saudi Iqama: Must be renewed 3 days before expiry. Employer initiates renewal. Check via Absher.sa app. Fine for 1st violation = Iqama renewal fee; 2nd = double the fee; 3rd violation = deportation. No per-day accumulation — but employer also faces penalties for late renewal. Source: moi.gov.sa official
✓ Qatar RP: Tied to employment contract. Check via Metrash2 app. Exit permit abolished in 2020 — you can leave freely without employer NOC.
⚠ Overstay Fines: UAE: AED 50/day (after grace period). Saudi: fine = Iqama renewal fee (not per-day — see Saudi fact above). Qatar Visit Visa: QAR 200/day. Qatar Residence Permit: QAR 10/day (max QAR 12,000). Overstay fines can block future visas and entry to all GCC countries. Sources: u.ae, moi.gov.sa official
✓ Passport Validity: Indian passport must have 6+ months validity for Gulf residence visa renewal. Renew passport before Iqama renewal if validity is low.
✓ Family Visa (Dependent): Spouse and children's visas are tied to the sponsor's Iqama. If the sponsor's Iqama lapses, all dependent visas become invalid automatically.
✗ India Visit During Renewal: If you are in India when your Iqama expires, you may need an Emergency Return Certificate from the Indian Embassy to re-enter your Gulf country legally.
